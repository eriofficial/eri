■ About Me ■

No stranger to the entertainment industry, ERI has been active on the stage and screen before delving into music in 2016.
Nurtured with the sounds of hip-hop and jazz from a young age, ERI's style can't be easily classified, jumping between old school Beats, house, Techno, and RnB. Like the melting pot of Southeast Asia, her sound takes inspiration from a variety of cultures.
I would say these people are some of my influences in life, to create my music; and they are Sade, Massive Attack, Erykah Badu, Little Dragon, Bats for Lashes, Chi-Lites and Left Eye.
